Protecting Your Privacy

At EveryTool.io, we believe in transparency and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information while using our suite of free tools.

Your trust is important to us - we never sell your data and minimize what we collect.

Last updated: June 24, 2025

EveryTool.io operates https://everytool.io (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our Site. Our Site provides a collection of free tools that process data entirely in your browser.

1. Data Collection

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about your interactions with our Site, such as page views, device information, and usage patterns, to improve our services. Google AdSense may collect data via cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

All data is anonymized and aggregated

2. Cookies

Our Site uses cookies to enhance functionality, analyze usage, and deliver ads. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie consent popup or your browser settings.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for Site functionality (e.g., navigation).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to track usage.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense for ad personalization.

3. Cookie Consent

We display a cookie consent popup to inform you about our use of cookies. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize preferences via the popup. Your choices are saved for future visits.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which may process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: For usage analytics.
  • Google AdSense: For ad delivery.

5. GDPR and CCPA Rights

If you are in the European Union/European Economic Area (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have specific rights:

  • GDPR: You may opt out of non-essential cookies via our consent popup, access your data, or request deletion by contacting us.
  • CCPA: California residents may opt out of data sales by selecting "Reject Non-Essential Cookies" in the popup or contacting us.

6. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us.
