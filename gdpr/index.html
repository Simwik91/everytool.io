GDPR Compliance

Learn how EveryTool.io's client-side architecture aligns with GDPR principles

Our Privacy-First Approach

At EveryTool.io, we've built our platform with privacy as the foundation. Unlike traditional web services, our tools run entirely within your browser - your data never touches our servers. This architecture fundamentally aligns with GDPR principles by design.

Client-Side Processing

All data processing happens in your browser using JavaScript

No Server Processing

Your files and data never leave your device

No Data Storage

We don't store any personal data or processed files

How Our Architecture Meets GDPR Requirements

Data Minimization

We collect absolutely no personal data through our tools. The only information we might receive is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form, which we use solely to respond to your inquiry.

Purpose Limitation

Since we don't collect or process personal data, there's no risk of using data beyond its original purpose. Any information you provide via our contact form is used exclusively to address your specific request.

Security by Design

By processing everything client-side, we eliminate server-related security risks. Your data remains entirely within your control and never leaves your browser.

Your Rights Under GDPR

While our architecture minimizes data collection, we fully respect your GDPR rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request any information we might have about you (limited to contact form submissions).
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of any information you've submitted through our contact form.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Since we don't process personal data beyond responding to contact requests, this right is inherently respected.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of any information you've submitted to us.

Data Processing Explanation

Our Tools

When you use any tool on EveryTool.io:

  • All processing occurs within your browser using JavaScript
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No cookies or tracking are used during tool operation
  • Your original files never leave your device
  • Processed results are generated and downloaded directly from your browser

Contact Form

If you choose to contact us through our form:

  • We collect only the information you provide (name, email, message)
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • We delete messages after 90 days unless required to retain them for legal reasons
  • We never use your contact information for marketing purposes

Contact Us

If you have questions about our GDPR compliance or wish to exercise your rights:

Use Our Contact Form

We respond to all legitimate requests within one month.
